Education

Sri Lanka's first and only student information management system with data on over 4.3 million students and over 10,000 schools. Working in collaboration with the Data Management Branch of the Ministry of Education (MoE), the objective of the project is to improve the planning, quality, delivery and outcomes of education across the country.

National Passenger Service Platform Project

The National Passenger Service Platform (NPSP) project involved the operation of a network of multimodal transport hubs around the Greater Colombo region (3K Project) as well as the provision of public with bus/ train-level connections, schedules, location and time of arrival/ departure.

LSF engaged to complete the initial high level design of the platform and helped develop the timetable dashboards used in the terminals.
